# Creating Markets

## Market Creation Requirements
- Connected wallet
- ETH for gas fees
- Clear, verifiable question
- Specific end date

## How to Create a Market

### Step 1: Navigate to Create
Click **Create Market** in the navigation

### Step 2: Enter Question
Write a clear, binary question (YES/NO answer)

**Good Examples:**
- "Will Bitcoin reach $100k by Dec 31, 2024?"
- "Will the Lakers win the NBA Championship?"

**Bad Examples:**
- "What will Bitcoin's price be?" (not binary)
- "Will it rain?" (not specific enough)

### Step 3: Set End Date
Choose when the market should close for trading

### Step 4: Add Category
Select the appropriate category (Crypto, Sports, Politics, etc.)

### Step 5: Review and Create
- Check all details
- Confirm gas fees
- Click **Create Market**

## Market Guidelines

### Question Requirements
- Must be binary (YES/NO)
- Must be objectively verifiable
- Must have a clear resolution date
- No ambiguous language

### Duration
- Minimum: 1 day
- Maximum: 1 year
- Consider event timing

### Categories
- **Crypto**: Cryptocurrency price predictions
- **Sports**: Game outcomes, championships
- **Politics**: Elections, policy decisions
- **Pop Culture**: Awards, releases, events
- **Other**: Everything else

## Fees
- Market creation: Gas fees only
- No platform fee for creation
- Creator earns fees from trades (coming soon)

## After Creation
- Market appears immediately
- Users can start trading
- You can trade in your own market
- Market resolves automatically after end date

## Best Practices
- Make questions specific and clear
- Set realistic end dates
- Choose appropriate categories
- Provide context if needed
