# Selling Shares

## When to Sell
- Lock in profits before market resolves
- Exit a position you no longer believe in
- Take advantage of probability changes

## How to Sell

### Step 1: View Your Positions
Navigate to **My Positions** or click on a market where you hold shares.

### Step 2: Select Shares to Sell
Choose the market and outcome (YES/NO) you want to sell.

### Step 3: Enter Quantity
Specify how many shares you want to sell (or click "Max").

### Step 4: Review Payout
Check the current market price and your expected payout.

### Step 5: Confirm
Click **Sell** and approve the transaction.

## Pricing
You receive the current market price for your shares:
- If YES is at 70%, you receive ~70¢ per YES share
- Prices fluctuate based on market sentiment

## Example
**Your Position**: 100 YES shares (bought at 60¢)
**Current Price**: 75¢ per YES share
**Sell Value**: $75
**Profit**: $15 (before gas)

## Important Notes
- You can sell anytime before market resolution
- Selling incurs gas fees
- Price may change between viewing and confirming
- Partial sells are allowed

## Strategies
- **Take Profit**: Sell when probability moves in your favor
- **Cut Losses**: Exit if market moves against you
- **Hold to Resolution**: Wait for maximum payout if confident
