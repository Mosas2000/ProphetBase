# ProphetBase User Guide

Welcome to ProphetBase, the premier prediction market on the Base network. This guide will help you navigate the platform, make predictions, and manage your portfolio.

## Getting Started

### 1. Connect Your Wallet
- Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top right corner.
- Supported wallets include **Coinbase Wallet**, **MetaMask**, and **Rainbow**.
- Ensure you are connected to the **Base Mainnet** (or Base Sepolia for testing).

### 2. Get Some Tokens
- You need **ETH** on Base for gas fees.
- You need **USDC** (or the platform's native token) to buy shares in markets.

## Exploring Markets

### Dashboard
The home page displays all active markets.
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific topics (e.g., "Bitcoin", "Election").
- **Filter**: Use the dropdown to sort by "Most Recent", "Ending Soon", or "Most Volume".
- **Categories**: Click on category pills to filter by topic.

### Market Details
Clicking on a market card opens its details.
- **Probability**: See the current market sentiment (e.g., 60% YES / 40% NO).
- **Chart**: View the price history over the last 24H or 7D.
- **Activity**: See real-time trades happening in this market.

## Trading

### Buying Shares
1. Select a Market.
2. Choose **YES** or **NO**.
3. Enter the amount you want to invest.
4. Review the potential payout.
5. Click **Buy** and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

### Selling Shares
Currently, you can hold shares until the market resolves. *Secondary market trading coming soon.*

## Market Resolution

- Markets automatically resolve via the oracle after the resolution date.
- If you hold winning shares, you can claim your winnings.
- **Winning Share Value**: Each winning share is redeemable for $1.00 (minus fees).

## Managing Your Portfolio

- **Watchlist**: Click the Star icon on any market to add it to your watchlist.
- **My Positions**: View all your active and closed positions in the Dashboard (coming soon).

## Troubleshooting

- **Transaction Failed**: Check you have enough ETH for gas.
- **Funds not showing**: Refresh the page or checking your wallet on BaseScan.
- **Contact Support**: Reach out on our Discord or via Twitter if issues persist.
